Privacy Practices Regarding Traveler Information

Effective December 31st, 2019

We respect your privacy rights

ITL World provides tools for corporate travel and online booking. ITL World recognizes the importance of protecting your personal information collected from our corporate clients and their employees, independent contractors, and other guests (“business travelers”). This Privacy Statement describes the personal information that we collect, and how that information may be used or disclosed by us in order to provide our services. Please note that we may update and amend this Privacy Statement from time to time.

Information we collect

Based on our agreements, ITL World obtains personal information from or on behalf of your employer, business partner, travel agency or travel arranger, when business travelers or travel arrangers submit data to us. Your employer, travel arranger or business partner is the controller of your personal information, and ITL World acts as a processor on their behalf. When we process personal information about you, we do so as necessary to provide the products you and your employer or travel sponsor use, to operate our business, to meet our contractual and legal obligations, to protect the security of our services, systems and customers, or to fulfill other legitimate interests as described in this Privacy Statement. If we book travel for your travel companions, we may collect similar information about them. The categories of personal information that we obtain include:

  • Account and profile information you, your employer or travel arranger provide to us: employer, country of residence, job title, employee identification number, name, surname, date of birth, gender, postal address, e-mail address, telephone number, credit card and payment information, travel and accommodation details, passport information, and special travel requests, such as a request for a wheelchair or a special meal, traveler preferences when provided, government-issued photo identification numbers, visa details, emergency contact information, fare and rate information, frequent flyer and loyalty card numbers, ticket number, itinerary details and information related to your requests.
  • Automatically collected information from your devices in connection with your online activity (including, in some cases, specific location information or information that enables us to identify you across devices). This includes, for example, your internet service provider’s address, your internet protocol or IP address, your operating system, your web browser software, the type of handheld or mobile device used to link to the web page, the language and time zone settings on your web browser, and a record of your activity or your “clickstream” while visiting ITL World.

Use of Information

We use your personal information to fulfil the following purposes in the context of performing a service for our corporate clients:

  • To process travel bookings.
  • To provide travel arrangers with access to travel information, to make and change travel reservations, to perform billing and accounting functions related to the travel, to perform internal business processes (such as testing and quality assurance), to perform and provide reporting functions for travel arrangers, and to issue tickets and other travel-related documents on behalf of travelers.
  • For reporting of corporate clients’ travel expenses.
  • For quality assurance and training purposes.
  • For the detection and prevention of fraudulent activities.
  • To manage budgets, travel policies, and relationship with our corporate clients and travel arrangers.
  • For communication of travel itineraries.
  • For compliance with our company policies and procedures, for accounting and financial purposes, to detect or prevent fraud or criminal activity, to perform, analyze and improve our business and services, to protect your property or the property of others, and as otherwise required by law.
  • To contact you and manage our relationship with you.
